**Domains:** jdmconnect.com.au + eligibility.jdmconnect.com.au
**Audit type:** Full site audit + cross-link strategy
**Date:** March 2026

---

## Executive Summary

JDM Connect has solid foundations — the homepage title is well-crafted, the site has good topical content (import guides, make-specific landing pages, FAQs), and the brand is clearly indexed and appearing in relevant searches. The **single biggest opportunity in the entire Australian JDM import space** is the ROVER Eligibility Checker at `eligibility.jdmconnect.com.au` — this tool contains 800+ MRE records and 600+ SEVS records in a fast, searchable interface that no competitor offers. Properly linked and optimised, it can rank for high-intent searches like "SEVS eligible cars Australia" and funnel that traffic directly to the main site.

The top three priorities are: (1) fix missing meta descriptions across every page — a 30-minute fix that immediately improves click-through rates from Google; (2) add an H1 and meta description to the eligibility tool and link it back to the main site with a strong CTA; (3) create model-specific landing pages for Toyota, Honda, Subaru, and Mitsubishi to match the existing Nissan page.

**Overall assessment: Good foundation — significant quick wins available, strong strategic moat with the eligibility tool.**

## Content Gap Analysis

### Gap 1 — SEVS Eligibility Hub (HIGH priority)
No competitor has a live, searchable SEVS/MRE tool. JDM Connect already built it. The gap is that it isn't discoverable — it has no SEO metadata and no inbound links from the main site.

**What to do:** Treat `eligibility.jdmconnect.com.au` as a content asset. Write a blog post on jdmconnect.com.au titled "How to Check if Your JDM Car is Eligible for Import to Australia" and embed a link/widget pointing to the tool. This creates a keyword-ranking article that also drives tool traffic.

**Format:** Blog post (600–800 words) + prominently linked tool
**Priority:** High | **Effort:** Quick win (2–3 hours)

---

### Gap 2 — Model-Specific Landing Pages (HIGH priority)
You have `/nissan/`. Competitors like J-Spec Imports and Tokyo Drive target model-level searches ("import Toyota Supra", "import Subaru WRX STI from Japan"). These pages rank well because intent is highly specific and competition is low.

**Missing pages to create:**
- `/toyota/` — Land Cruiser 70/80 series, Supra A80, Chaser, Mark II
- `/honda/` — NSX, Integra Type R, Civic Type R, S2000
- `/subaru/` — WRX STI, Forester STI, Legacy GT
- `/mitsubishi/` — Lancer Evolution VI/VII/VIII, Pajero Evolution
- `/mazda/` — RX-7 FD, MX-5 NA/NB, RX-8

**Format:** Make landing pages (~600 words) + popular model cards
**Priority:** High | **Effort:** Moderate (half-day per page)

---

### Gap 3 — Import Cost Guide / Calculator (HIGH priority)
"How much does it cost to import a JDM car to Australia?" is one of the most searched queries in this space and JDM Connect has no dedicated page for it. Competitors like ZervTek and dreamx.au have guides ranking for this.

**What to create:** A detailed cost breakdown page covering auction price, buyer's fee, shipping (RoRo vs container), quarantine, compliance (SEVS/RAW), duty, GST, registration, delivery. Optionally add a simple interactive cost estimator.

**Format:** Long-form guide (1,200–1,500 words) + cost table
**Priority:** High | **Effort:** Moderate (half-day)

---

### Gap 4 — "25-Year Rule" Explainer (MEDIUM priority)
The 25-year import rule is a major search topic ("25 year rule car import Australia") and JDM Connect has no dedicated content for it. It's low competition and high intent.

**Format:** Blog post (800–1,000 words)
**Priority:** Medium | **Effort:** Quick win (2 hours)

---

### Gap 5 — "MRE vs SEVS" Explainer (MEDIUM priority)
Many buyers don't understand the difference between MRE (Model Report Entry) and SEVS pathways. No clear explainer exists anywhere in readable form. This is a question-based keyword opportunity: "what is the difference between MRE and SEVS Australia."

**Format:** Blog post or FAQ addition
**Priority:** Medium | **Effort:** Quick win (1–2 hours)

---

### Gap 6 — Model-Specific Eligibility Articles (MEDIUM priority)
High-intent long-tail: "is the Nissan Skyline R34 legal in Australia", "can I import an EVO IX to Australia", "is the Toyota JZX100 Chaser eligible for import". JDM Connect's eligibility tool has the data — it just needs content pages built around popular models.

**Format:** Model-specific blog posts
**Priority:** Medium | **Effort:** Quick win (1 hour each)

---

### Gap 7 — Japanese Auction Explained (LOW priority)
"How does Japanese car auction work" is informational but builds trust and captures top-of-funnel traffic. Tokyo Drive has content here; JDM Connect doesn't.

**Format:** Blog post (800 words)
**Priority:** Low | **Effort:** Moderate

## Cross-Link Strategy: Main Site ↔ Eligibility Tool

The eligibility tool is currently an island — Google can find it but it has no connection to the main site and no reason to rank. Here's how to tie them together:

### On eligibility.jdmconnect.com.au (add these)
1. **Sticky "Get an Import Quote" CTA banner** — top or bottom of the tool with link back to `jdmconnect.com.au/import-process/`. Text: *"Found a car you like? JDM Connect handles the whole import → Get a free quote"*
2. **Footer link** — add "© JDM Connect — Japan Car Import Specialists" with link to main site
3. **Meta description** — *"Search 1,400+ approved vehicles on Australia's SEVS and MRE eligibility registers. Instantly check if your JDM car can be imported. Powered by JDM Connect."*
4. **H1 tag** — "ROVER Eligibility Checker — Australian SEVS & MRE Import Register"
5. **Open Graph tags** — for social sharing: title, description, image (so it looks good when shared in JDM Facebook groups/forums)

### On jdmconnect.com.au (add these)
1. **Homepage widget/section** — "Is Your Car Eligible? Check the SEVS & MRE Register →" with a button linking to the eligibility tool. Position it between the hero and services sections.
2. **Blog post** — "How to Check if Your Car is SEVS Eligible in Australia" (700 words) — ranks for the keyword AND drives traffic to the tool
3. **FAQ addition** — "How do I know if my JDM car is eligible for import?" → answer includes link to tool
4. **Import Process page** — add a "Step 1: Check Eligibility" section with link to tool
5. **Sidebar/footer link** on all blog posts — "Check vehicle eligibility →"

### Content to write (ranked by impact)
1. "How to Check if Your Car is SEVS Eligible in Australia" — blog post, links to tool
2. "MRE vs SEVS: What's the Difference?" — blog post, links to tool + process page
3. "How Much Does It Cost to Import a JDM Car to Australia in 2025?" — pillar page
4. Model-specific pages: /toyota/, /honda/, /subaru/, /mitsubishi/
